Explore Swat

Swat Valley is known for green mountains, rivers, waterfalls, historical sites, and cool weather. It is a favorite destination for nature lovers, families, and adventure seekers.

Malam Jabba

Malam Jabba is a famous hill station and winter sports destination in Swat. It attracts visitors for skiing, chair lifts, mountain views, and peaceful weather during summer and winter seasons.

Mingora Bazaar

Mingora Bazaar is an active commercial area where visitors can explore local shops, traditional items, food, and daily life in Swat. It gives tourists a chance to experience the culture of the valley.

Swat Museum

Swat Museum contains historical objects, Buddhist artifacts, sculptures, coins, and cultural items. It is important for understanding the ancient history and heritage of the region.
