Explore Lahore

Lahore is one of Pakistan's most historic and lively cities. It is famous for Mughal-era buildings, gardens, traditional food, literature, and colorful festivals. The city reflects a strong connection between Pakistan's past and present.

Badshahi Mosque

The Badshahi Mosque is one of Lahore's most iconic landmarks. Its red sandstone structure, large courtyard, and elegant domes make it a major attraction for tourists, students, and history lovers.

Lahore Fort

Lahore Fort is a UNESCO-recognized historical site located near the Badshahi Mosque. It contains palaces, halls, gates, and decorative artwork that show the strength of Mughal design and royal lifestyle.

Shalimar Gardens

Shalimar Gardens are known for their terraces, fountains, trees, and peaceful walkways. They were designed as a royal garden and remain a popular place to understand Lahore's garden culture.
