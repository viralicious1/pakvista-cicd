Explore Karachi

Karachi is Pakistan's largest city and an important center of trade, culture, and entertainment. Its beaches, museums, food streets, and historical places make it a dynamic tourist destination.

Clifton Beach

Clifton Beach is one of Karachi's most popular seaside locations. Families and tourists visit the beach for walks, horse rides, camel rides, and sunset views along the Arabian Sea.

Mohatta Palace

Mohatta Palace is a beautiful historical building that now serves as a museum and cultural venue. Its design, stonework, and exhibitions attract visitors interested in history, architecture, and art.

Pakistan Maritime Museum

The Pakistan Maritime Museum displays naval history, ships, submarines, aircraft, and maritime artifacts. It is a useful educational place for students, families, and visitors who want to learn about Pakistan's naval heritage.
